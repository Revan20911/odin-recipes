Turkey Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1-1 1/2 lbs of ground turkey
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp of Worchestershire Sauce
  • 2 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2 tsp of onion powder
  • 2 tsp of salt and pepper


Directions

  1. In a large bowl mix meat, egg, and spices together until thuroughly mixed
  2. In a large pan pour 1 Tbsp of olive oil and turn heat on to high for 45 seconds then medium
  3. Divide the meat into 4 equal sized balls and use a spatula to flatten them,
  4. Put the flattened patties onto the pan and cook each side for 4 minutes or until golden brown on both sides
